ABOUT US

Sindika is a cost effective, professional, corporate event management company and events, promotion, decoration, design, fabrication and installation specialist, based in Dubai Media City Free Zone, and operating in/out of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Sindika is dedicated to providing creative event solutions well known for our innovative concepts in presenting brands and companies at corporate events.

We are engaged in delivering an event that guests will remember long after it ends. Whether it’s a private or business function, a small gathering of friends or a gala for thousands, we will work with different suppliers & artists from all over the world to create a beautiful and unique experience. We can arrange as many or as few services as the client needs, on time within his budget.

Our commitment to ongoing professional development means we can bring you fresh, new ideas as well as keeping abreast of safety and legislative issues. We spend time finding new and creative ways to add sparkle to your event. Let us bring you the latest trends. Attention to detail can make or break an event.

Why Us?

Working with Sindika is a unique experience in that we can handle every aspect of your event, from concept development and logistics to event production to ground transportation. We are a team of experts who can effectively budget, design, source, contract, produce and execute programs that provide creative solutions and results to exceed your expectations. We also gladly work with in-house planners and provide a strong support and enhancement to your existing team.
